About Us

Have you ever come back after a tiring day hoping to relax at home but instead of providing you with refuge, your home seems to be yelling at you - "Please fix that rotten window and I told you that gutter is clogged and you haven't had the Air Conditioner tuned for summer and yes, what happened to your promise of a manicured lawn?"

Agavey is formed by a few no-gooders who own homes but are still learning how to manage and maintain them effectively. Their home often hurls questions like these at them . They have shared your struggle of spending hours on grueling research and multiple estimates and yet being unsure if they have signed up for best quality service at the best price.

Agavey was founded to ease the pain of home maintenance and improvement. Agavey is a company that connects home owners to high quality verified service providers/contractors/retailers while also obtaining significant price reductions for the home owners.

Our goals is simple:

  • Create an efficient marketplace for home improvement and maintenance services
  • Provide a selection of high quality services and service providers
  • Provide price discounts to home owners through efficiency and selectivity

We believe in exceptional experience, right price and high quality while identifying the best vendors and services for home owners.
